# Project Brief

## Positioning

Market Signal Studio is a finance-content operating system for creators who want to explain market moves with data, not scrape headlines into templated videos.

The brand promise:

> Here are the market stories that actually moved prices, and here is the evidence.

## First niche

Start narrow:

- AI stocks and semiconductor supply chain.
- Fed, rates, inflation, and market reactions.
- Single-stock "why it moved" stories backed by filings, issuer releases, or official data.

The first recurring format should be "The Story Everyone Saw vs. The Data Everyone Missed." It creates an original angle and gives the engine a clear scoring target.

## MVP workflow

1. Ingest official, first-party, and discovery sources.
2. Archive source snapshots for auditability.
3. Normalize items into a canonical source schema.
4. Map tickers, entities, sectors, and themes.
5. Cluster duplicate coverage around one event.
6. Score the story by market impact, novelty, authority, timeliness, corroboration, explainability, and buzz.
7. Generate an editorial package: format, style variant, hook, summary, why it matters, chart idea, caveat, caption, thumbnail text, script.
8. Run compliance QA, source-rights review, claim traceability, platform-readiness checks, and approval readiness.
9. Human editor approves with notes when warnings remain, edits, records or generates voice, and renders the final asset.

## Non-goals for v0

- Fully automated posting.
- Raw article republishing.
- Raw real-time quote redistribution.
- Personalized investment advice.
- Buy, sell, or price-target recommendations.

## Success metrics

- Story acceptance rate.
- False-positive story rate.
- Time from signal to draft.
- QA revision count.
- Completion rate and saves per published video.
- Newsletter signups per 1,000 views.
